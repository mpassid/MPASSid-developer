    Authentication

    We use your Ethereum address for authentication. For detailed information how to authenticate with our API, see our tutorial.

    Grab your bearer token from SpatialIndex and paste “Bearer xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx” into the Authorize button below.

    Click “foam” below to explore the API. You can also expand the “Models” to explore the model defined by our API. A simple way to interact with the API is to load the definition into Postman or Insomnia for example.
